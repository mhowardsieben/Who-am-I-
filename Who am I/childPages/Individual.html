Definition

Individual Identity is how one identifys their self, this can be influenced by both internal and external factors.

As a gamer

I love video games, they're a love that I occasionally need a break from, but I never the less love them. My two favourite games also represent my two favourite genres. In one corner Crusader Kings 2 representing historical strategy, and in the other corner CS;GO representing competitive shooters. Crusader Kings 2 is actually the main game responsible for my love of history, it introduced me to a whole genre of historical games from the company paradox interactive. CS;GO is honestly just plain fun and different from anything from anything on the market. This is due to it's unique style of firing your weapon, instead of having you look down a scope, CS;GO gives each gun a unique spray pattern they must follow to hit their shots. It also set the precedent for competitive shooters to be 5v5. In short I play video games for fun, relaxation, and a challenge.
